Executive Summary

VeilMatch is an innovative, secure, and anonymous dating platform leveraging Web3 technology to revolutionize how people connect and find love. It prioritizes user privacy, safety, and anonymity while fostering meaningful relationships through blockchain, encryption, and AI-powered matchmaking.

Vision and Mission

Vision: Create a global platform for meaningful connections built on trust, compatibility, and respect, with a focus on privacy and security.

Mission: Redefine online dating by integrating Web3 technology, ensuring secure and anonymous interactions, and empowering users with control over their data.

Key Features

  • Anonymous Profiles
  • Decentralized Matchmaking
  • Advanced Safety Tools
  • Web3 Rewards
  • Global Reach
  • AI Matchmaking
  • User Control

Technology Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React/Vue.js

Backend: Node.js or Python

Blockchain: Solana, Ton, Xion, Polygon

Database: MongoDB, IPFS, Arweave

AI/ML: TensorFlow, Scikit-learn

Security: bcrypt, Argon2, SSL/TLS

Token Integration

Blockchain: Solana, Ton, Xion, Polygon

Total Supply: 1,000,000 VMT
